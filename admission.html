ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

  1. Incoming Freshmen
    • Scast examination result
    • Form 138-1A with the original signature of the principal/high school report card
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
    • Medical Certificate from any government physician
    • 4pcs recent 2x2 identical ID picture with white background
    • Certified NSO Birth Certificate (original and photocopy)
    • Duly accomplished Guidance Information Form
    • 1 long brown envelope for Guidance Office
    • 1 window envelope
    • 1 mailing stamp

  2. Transferees
    • Scast examination result
    • Certificate of Transfer of Credential/Honorable Dismissal
    • Informative copy of Transcript of Records
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
    • Medical Certificate from any government physician
    • 4pcs recent 2x2 identical ID picture with white background
    • Certified NSO Birth Certificate (original and photocopy)
    • Duly accomplished Guidance Information Form
    • 1 long brown envelope for Guidance Office
    • 1 window envelope
    • 1 mailing stamp

  3. Old Students
    • Duly accomplished clearance
    • Grades from previous semester
    • Medical Certificate from any government physician (first term only)
    • Scholarship requirement (grade maintenance for scholars)
    • Updated Guidance Information Form
    • 1 mailing stamp

  4. Foreign Students
    • Scast examination result
    • Certificate of completion of a secondary curriculum
    • Original Transcript of Records
    • Personal data profile
    • Affidavit of support from respective government
    • Alien Certificate of Registration
    • Certificate of Proficiency in English based on TOEFL/IELTS score (for non-native speakers of English) and student visa
