    TESTING REQUIREMENTS

    1. For Incoming Freshmen:
      A certification from the school principal/teacher-in-charge that he/she is a high school graduating student;
    2. For Transferees:
      Transfer credentials or a certificate of grades dule signed by the school registrar;
    3. For Out-of-School Youth:
      Form 138-A or high school report card
